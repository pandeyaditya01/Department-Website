EdXBVU

A web application developed, designed and hosted by the students and faculty of the department of Electrical and Computer Engineering on Cloud. The objective of this application is to ease the gap between teaching and learning. The application features access to video streaming, data upload, projects, publications, student data, class notes etc.

Department faculties can make use of this platform to demonstrate their courses along with a reliable platform to store and retrieve important academic files on a single platform. edXBVU is a repository for teaching, learning, data storage, social media and online events with Identity Access Management.
